Introduction

Hello! Our team is composed of five students from Northeastern University majoring in computer science. We are Ange, Zahra, Saidah, Sania, and Tina (left to right). As our first hackathon project together, we wanted to highlight the inequalities that women face as a result of urban design, specifically in major cities. Our hopes for this project are to spread awareness surrounding the gender data gap and foster conversation about societal gender norms.
